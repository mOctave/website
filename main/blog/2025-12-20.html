Flashbang Time

20 December 2025

So, did I say in October that I was sort of happy with this website?

How very funny of me.

Anyways, all that to say that now instead of having your eyes bleed from bad design, you can have them bleed from slightly less bad but much brighter design. More changes should be around here soonish, and I may even get down to work writing something her other than a boring post about website changes soon.
