Taskship: Reborn

16 January 2026

What if people actually looked at your ship and gave you appropriate jobs? What if you could turn a profit using your old Flivver for hauling joyriders up to orbit, or join mock battles in your Osprey to thrill the galaxy's dedicated 31st-century-television-equivalent viewers? Well, now you can.

When I first started work on Taskship, it was a proof-of-concept way to soft-balance ships and make traditionally niche options more appealing. Then I expanded it to cover all human ships. And then it sat in a corner collecting dust for two years.

Now, Taskship is back. With several bug and typo fixes, a new icon, and plans to add more tasks for more ships, this release marks the beginning of what will hopefully be a long run of Taskship releases to come.

You can get the latest version of the plugin here. It should also be available in the official plugin directory in a day or two.

If you find a problem with the plugin or have suggestions of jobs to add, please open an issue or ping me on the Endless Sky Discord server.

Enjoy!
